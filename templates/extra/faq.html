FAQ

  1. What is Freelancer?

    Freelancer is an online platform that connects skilled workers with service-demanded users.

  2. How does Freelancer work?

    Service providers create a skills profile that showcases their technical capabilities, while end users create service request profiles that describe the task type and requirements. The system suggests the most matched service provider based on both profiles. Users can communicate via integrated messenger, rate the service providers' quality, and share a provider with other users to lower costs.

  3. What kind of services can I find on Freelancer?

    Freelancer offers a wide range of services, including programming, web development, graphic design, writing, data entry, and more.

  4. How do I sign up for Freelancer?

    You can sign up for Freelancer by using your Google account.

  5. How do I create a service request on Freelancer?

    To create a service request, you need to describe the task type, requirements, and time frame in the form of user stories and technical constraints.

  6. How do I find a suitable service provider on Freelancer?

    You can search for a service provider by keywords, category, user, or programming language. The system suggests the most matched service provider based on your service request profile.

  7. How do I communicate with my service provider on Freelancer?

    You can communicate with your service provider via integrated messenger, which supports text messages and voice calls.

  8. How do I rate the quality of my service provider on Freelancer?

    You can rate the quality of your service provider by leaving a review and rating on their profile.

  9. How does Freelancer ensure the quality of its service providers?

    Freelancer verifies the skills and qualifications of its service providers through their skills profile and portfolio.

  10. How can I invite my friends to join Freelancer?

    You can invite your friends to join Freelancer by sending them an email invitation.

  11. How can I customize my settings on Freelancer?

    You can customize your settings on Freelancer by choosing the types of application category you like to develop, the technologies you want to use, and the publishing framework.

  12. How can I update my profile information on Freelancer?

    You can update your profile information on Freelancer by going to your profile settings and making the necessary changes.

  13. How can I get help with my Freelancer account?

    You can get help with your Freelancer account by checking out the Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) or contacting the support team.

  14. Can I cancel a service request or a service provider's bid on Freelancer?

    Yes, you can cancel a service request or a service provider's bid on Freelancer at any time.

  15. How do I report a service provider or an end user on Freelancer?

    You can report a service provider or an end user on Freelancer by using the reporting feature and providing relevant details.

  16. How can I build a good profile as a service provider on Freelancer?

    To build a good profile as a service provider on Freelancer, you should showcase your technical capabilities, provide a portfolio of your previous work, and maintain good communication with your clients.

  17. How can I increase my chances of getting hired as a service provider on Freelancer?

    To increase your chances of getting hired as a service provider on Freelancer, you should build a strong skills profile, bid competitively, and provide high-quality work to your clients.

  18. How can I provide feedback on my experience with Freelancer?

    You can provide feedback on your experience with Freelancer by leaving a review and rating on the platform or by contacting the support team.
